Frequently Asked Questions

How does GreenImpact calculate my environmental impact? +
GreenImpact uses data from your daily activities, such as transportation, energy usage, and waste, combined with standard emission factors from reliable sources like the EPA to estimate your carbon footprint.
Is my data secure? +
Yes, we prioritize your privacy. All data is encrypted and stored securely, and we never share your personal information without consent. Check our Privacy Policy for details.
How can I reset my password? +
Click "Forgot password?" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the reset email we send you.
What subscription plans are available? +
We offer free basic tracking and premium plans with advanced analytics, personalized tips, and community features. Visit our pricing page for more info.
How do I delete my account? +
Log in, go to account settings, and select "Delete Account." Note that this action is permanent and all your data will be erased.
