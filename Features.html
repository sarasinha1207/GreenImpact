Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the AI insights? +
Our AI uses data from reliable sources like EPA and IPCC, combined with machine learning models trained on vast datasets for high accuracy.
Can I integrate with other apps? +
Yes, GreenImpact integrates with fitness trackers, smart home devices, and more for seamless data import.
Is GreenImpact free? +
Basic features are free, with premium options for advanced analytics and unlimited offsets.
How do carbon offsets work? +
We partner with verified projects to remove or reduce equivalent CO2 from the atmosphere for every ton you offset.
What data do you collect? +
Only anonymized activity data to provide insights. We prioritize privacy and comply with GDPR.
